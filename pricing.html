Economics - Cost of Production Calculator

​The Cost of Production Calculator will help you evaluate the costs and returns of crops.

Below is a cost of production calculator to help you estimate the costs and returns of corn and soybean cropping systems. To run the calculator, you can either use 1) predetermined variable costs based on figures calculated by Iowa State Extension or 2) your own variable costs or figures calculated by your nearest college of agriculture. Simply select an option below to get started.

  1. Input your expected "Market Prices."
  2. Input expected "Planted Acres" per crop.
  3. Input your "Expected Yield" for all 3 cropping systems.
